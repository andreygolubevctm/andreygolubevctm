• Your first payment will be deducted within five (5) working days which will activate your new HIF membership.
  • Once your membership has been successfully activated, HIF will send a welcome email to your nominated email address. The welcome email will confirm all the details of your new health insurance policy, so please take a few minutes to read it carefully.
  • You will also receive a courtesy follow-up call from a HIF Sales Consultant to confirm your details and answer any questions you may have.
  • All future premiums will be deducted based on the payment frequency you have selected (fortnightly, monthly, quarterly, half-yearly or yearly).
  • Changes to payment frequency and payment method can be made after purchase confirmation from comparethemarket.com.au.
