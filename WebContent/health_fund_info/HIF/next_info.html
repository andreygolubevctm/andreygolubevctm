• Your first payment will be deducted within 10 working days in order to activate your new HIF membership.
  • Once your membership has been successfully activated, HIF will send a welcome email to your nominated email address. The welcome email will confirm all the details of your new HIF health insurance policy, so please take a few minutes to read it carefully.
  • All future premiums will be deducted based on the payment frequency you have selected (fortnightly, monthly, quarterly, half-yearly or yearly).
  • Changes to payment frequency and/or payment method can be arranged directly with HIF after confirming your purchase from comparethemarket.com.au
  • Important: If you are switching to HIF and still have a direct debit arrangement in place with your previous fund, we suggest that you cancel the arrangement as soon as possible. You may be able to do this through your previous fund's online member service area.
