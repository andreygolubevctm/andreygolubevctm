Westfund is the eighth largest open health fund in Australia, providing peace of mind with affordable private health insurance Australia wide. Their members are both owners and members of the fund, with profits returned to them through loyalty rewards, premium discounts and improvements to products and services.

Westfund's Member Advantages program provides additional benefits which accrue over time, so the longer you're with them, the more they give you back. Depending on your selected cover, Member Advantages can include Sunglasses, Travel Expenses, Hospital Top Up, Overnight+, Surgery+, Premium Pause and Premium Discounts as well as Bonus Benefits such as braces, surgical treatment by podiatrists and CPAP masks.
