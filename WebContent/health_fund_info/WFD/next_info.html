• A Westfund Sales Consultant will contact you within 3-5 working days
  • The Westfund Consultant will check your details, answer any questions you may have, discuss your claim options and advise you of your first payment.
  • You will also receive by email and post an application form and rebate form (if applicable) for your signature. It is important to return this paperwork promptly.
  • Once Westfund has received these forms, they will issue you with a New Member Pack and membership card.
  • Please note that your first payment will be the regular contribution plus an amount to cover the time between the start date and the payment deduction date.
