CBHS is not-for-profit and has been awarded the 2012 Private Health Insurer of the Year by Roy Morgan Research. Formed in 1951, CBHS has provided ultimate peace of mind to its members and has grown to become Australia's 3rd largest restricted Private Health Insurer covering over 180,000 people. It is an exclusive private health insurer for current and former employees, contractors and franchisees of the Commonwealth Bank Group (which includes Commonwealth Bank of Australia, Commsec, Comminsure, the Colonial Group of companies and Bankwest) and their eligible family members (partner, parents, siblings [their partners and children], children and grandchildren).
